3-INCH PULLEY CLAMP — CH-PU05

1.8mm steel clamp for 3-inch residential sectional door pulley assemblies. Secures the pulley to the track hardware at the correct position for cable routing.

Pulley Series CH-PU05

3” Pulley Clamp

Steel pulley clamp designed to mount and secure a 3-inch cable pulley to the vertical track bracket on residential sectional door systems. The 1.8mm steel construction provides the rigidity needed to keep the pulley in fixed alignment under the dynamic cable loads of door operation. The clamp locks the pulley axle at the correct height and angle to guide the lift cable cleanly from the door bottom bracket up toward the cable drum. Stocked individually for service and replacement work. Pairs with CH-PU01, CH-PU03, and other 3-inch pulley variants in the CH-PU series.
