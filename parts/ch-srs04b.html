USA Insulation Panel Side Seal 40mm

PVC side seal with extruded aluminum profile carrier designed for USA-specification 40mm insulated sectional door panels. The aluminum extrusion provides dimensional stability along the full door height, preventing the seal from sagging or shifting over time. The flexible PVC wiper element maintains consistent contact with the door jamb stop, blocking drafts, moisture, and air infiltration at the door sides. Properly sealing the sides of insulated sectional doors is critical to preserving the door's thermal performance and reducing HVAC load in conditioned garages.
