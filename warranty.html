Warranty & Quality Policy.

We stand behind every part we supply.

Section 1

Parts & Hardware — 90-Day Warranty

All parts and hardware supplied by Revuelt Garages carry a 90-day limited warranty from the date of purchase.

Covers:

  • Defective material
  • Manufacturing defects present at time of purchase

Does Not Cover:

  • Improper installation
  • Modification or alteration of the part
  • Normal wear and tear

Section 2

Custom Doors (When Available) — 1-Year Limited Warranty

Custom garage door orders, when available through Revuelt, carry a 1-year limited warranty.

Covers:

  • Structural integrity defects under normal use conditions

Does Not Cover:

  • Installation by unqualified or unlicensed technicians
  • Use outside of manufacturer specifications

⚠ Torsion Spring Safety Warning

Garage door torsion springs are under extreme tension and can cause severe injury or death if mishandled or installed incorrectly. Springs must be installed by a qualified, licensed garage door technician. Revuelt supplies springs to trade professionals only.

Section 4

General Quality Policy

Revuelt sources commercial-grade parts from vetted manufacturers. If a part fails due to a manufacturing defect within the warranty period, we will replace it.

Contact us with your order details and a description of the issue. We review all warranty claims within 1 business day.

Questions about a part or warranty claim?

We respond same-day to all trade account inquiries.
